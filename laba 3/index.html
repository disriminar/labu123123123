Juul - the system is simple and concise, there are no buttons or other controls. The rectangular battery pack is made of a weightless aluminum alloy and weighs only 15 grams when combined with an unused cartridge. Device dimensions: 95 mm x 15 mm x 7 mm. On the front plane there is an indicator light that glows during use to reflect the tightening force and when charging the device. For charging, Juul is inserted with its lower part, on which the contact group is located, into the socket of a magnetic docking station, which, in turn, is connected via a USB connector to any device or power bank connected to the network.
